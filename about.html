About

I'm a UX Researcher with a background in cognitive science and Human-Computer Interaction research. Most recently I led user research on the Benetech Labs' Team and its projects. I also do consulting with smaller startups.

Prior to Benetech, I conducted research with the Human-Robot Interaction Lab and ASSIST Lab at the University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC).
